Case Study

Enhancing Industrial Efficiency and Decision-making with Powerful Dashboards and IoT Integration

Client Overview

Our client is a leading industrial company seeking to optimize their operations, maintenance, and engineering processes for increased efficiency and better decision-making. They have diverse assets such as pumps, tanks, and other equipment critical to their operations. The client's goal is to harness the power of IoT and Business Intelligence (BI) to monitor and manage these assets more effectively, ensuring smooth operations and reduced downtime.

Project Summary

Our team worked closely with the client to develop a comprehensive solution that includes Business Intelligence, Operation & Maintenance (O&M), Engineering, Pump, and Tank Dashboards. These dashboards provide real-time insights into asset performance, critical parameters, and operational efficiency. Furthermore, we integrated PTC Thingworx and KepServerEx to enable seamless data acquisition and analytics, elevating their capabilities to the next level.

Challenges Faced

  • Diverse Asset Management: Managing multiple assets, including pumps, tanks, and associated equipment, required creating specialized dashboards to cater to unique monitoring needs for each asset type.
  • Alarm Management: Implementing a flexible and user-friendly alarm configuration system allows users to set custom thresholds for various parameters and receive real-time alerts when these thresholds are breached.
  • Real-time Analytics: Developing advanced data visualization techniques and real-time analytics to provide the client with actionable insights and the ability to make data-driven decisions promptly.

Solution Provided

1. Pump Dashboard:

  • Configurable Alarms: Users can set alarms for parameters like temperature, triggering alerts when the threshold is exceeded.
  • Comparative Analysis: A bar chart shows Daily Pump Start Count vs. Total Run Hours for easy performance comparison.
  • Comprehensive Pump Data: The dashboard displays Pump Run Signal, Seal/Bearing/Motor Temperature, and Vibration Spectrum charts. Additionally, it presents Pump Status (On/Off), Max Temperature, Speed, Total Run Hours in a Year, and Pump Start Count in a Day.

2. Tank Dashboard:

  • Customizable Alarms: Like the Pump Dashboard, users can set alarms based on parameters such as temperature.
  • Detailed Tank Information: The dashboard provides essential details like Diameter, Set Point, Gross Volume, Usable Gross Volume, Net Volume, Usable Net Volume, Height, Volume, Density, and Temperature. Additionally, it displays Tank Status (On/Off).
  • Hourly Average Visualization: A bar chart shows the Hourly Average of Gross Volume to help monitor trends.

3. Business Intelligence Dashboard:

  • Uplift and Turn Analysis: This dashboard presents monthly uplift, a pie chart of Pump Turns and Tank Turns, and a line chart of Net Volume for the Tank Dashboard.

4. Engineering Dashboard:

  • Performance Metrics: The dashboard includes Bar Hourly Average Charts of Flow Rate to Ramp, Current Usable Volume of All Tanks, Seal & Bearing Temperature, Current Days Reserve, Daily Peak Flow Rate, Uplift, and Pump Run Timers.

5. O&M Dashboard:

  • Comprehensive Status Overview: This dashboard displays the status of all Pumps and Tanks, as well as critical properties like Filters, Alarms, System Pressure, Generator Status, Pipeline Flow Pressure, and Receipt.

IoT Integration - PTC Thingworx and KepServerEx

  • Installation and Integration: We successfully installed Thingworx and KepServerEx and ensured seamless integration.
  • Responsive Mashups: Developed responsive mashups for all dashboards, providing an intuitive and user-friendly device experience.
  • Industrial Things: Created and mapped Industrial & Remote Things with Generic Things to establish a robust IoT ecosystem.
  • Data Archiving: Implemented data archiving to Azure Blob Storage for analytics, optimizing data storage and retrieval.

Results and Impact

By implementing our comprehensive solution, our client experienced remarkable improvements in their industrial efficiency and decision-making capabilities:

  • Real-time Insights: The dashboards provided real-time insights into asset performance, enabling proactive maintenance, and reducing downtime.
  • Data-Driven Decision-making: Armed with actionable data, the client made informed decisions to optimize processes and maximize productivity.
  • Alarming System: The customizable flawed system ensures quick response to critical events, preventing potential hazards and costly failures.
  • Enhanced Industrial IoT: The integration of PTC Thingworx and KepServerEx established a robust IoT platform, enabling data-driven automation and analytics.
  • Scalability: The solution's scalability allowed the client to add more assets and quickly expand the IoT ecosystem.

Conclusion

Our client's journey from relying on manual monitoring to harnessing the power of IoT and Business Intelligence resulted in increased operational efficiency, reduced downtime, and better decision-making capabilities. The comprehensive dashboards and IoT integration empowered them to stay ahead in the competitive industrial landscape, positioning them as a true industry leader.

Empowering Manufacturing and Automotive Industries with Integrated Thingworx and Windchill Solution

Introduction

In pursuit of enhancing efficiency and decision-making in the manufacturing and automotive sectors, a leading technology expert undertook a transformative project by combining the powerful capabilities of Thingworx and Windchill. This case study explores how the integration of these platforms resulted in the development of four interactive dashboards, each uniquely designed to streamline processes, improve data accessibility, and drive data-driven insights.

Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were to:

  • Facilitate seamless data retrieval for operators by dynamically searching and filtering part information.
  • Empower users with comprehensive insights into part structures, revisions, versions, and release states.
  • Enhance cost estimation accuracy and provide valuable data visualization for better decision-making.

Solution

Part Explorer Dashboard:

Part Information and EMS Filtering

This dashboard enabled operators to quickly retrieve part-related data and filter information based on the selection of EMS (Electronic Manufacturing Services).

Key features included:

  • Part Search: Operators could input a part number, and the system would fetch data from the Request for Quotation (RFQ) file stored in the Postgres DB.
  • EMS Filtering: A dropdown menu allowed users to select a specific EMS, dynamically filtering the table data based on the chosen EMS.
  • Data Visualization: The table displayed essential details such as Part Cost, Part Supplier/EMS, Part Description, Part Max Price, and Min Price from the RFQ file.
  • Price Date Highlighting: Data older than four months appeared with a red dot in a separate column, while recent data within the last four months were marked with a green color, facilitating quick identification.

Part Explorer Dashboard:

Part Bill of Materials (BOM) and Insights

This dashboard empowered users to explore and visualize part structures obtained from Windchill, along with valuable insights.

Notable features included:

  • BOM Search: Users could search for a part in Windchill, and the system would display its comprehensive bill of materials, including Part Revision, Version, and Release State.
  • Hardware References: Parts with hardware references were displayed in a separate table for easy reference and analysis.
  • Missing Prices: For parts with incomplete pricing information, a dedicated table highlighted the lost data, ensuring prompt attention.
  • COGS and MTBF Views: The dashboard provided two views for Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) and Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF), allowing users to assess cost and reliability metrics.

Price Estimation Dashboard:

Price Estimation and Search

This dashboard facilitated the insertion of estimated part prices and allowed users to search for specific part estimated prices.

Key functionalities included:

  • Price Estimation: Users could input estimated prices for valid parts, ensuring accurate data entry.
  • Data Validation: The system allowed only valid parts of data to be inserted, maintaining data integrity.
  • Estimated Price Table: A separate table displayed all estimated prices entered by users, aiding in monitoring and analysis.
  • Part Price Search: Users could search for estimated prices of specific parts, providing quick access to crucial data.

Data Upload Dashboard:

Data Upload and Visualization

This dashboard enabled users to upload sheets containing part and supplier information, and upon uploading, it displayed comprehensive part details.

Key features included:

  • Data Upload: Users could upload sheets with Part Name, Description, Quantity, Price, Supplier, and Lead Time information.
  • Data Visualization: The dashboard presented a table with all uploaded part details, including the total cost (Price x Quantity).
  • COGS and MTBF Views: The dashboard offered two views for Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) and Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF), aiding in analyzing key performance metrics.

Benefits

The integrated Thingworx and Windchill solution provided numerous benefits for the company:

  • Streamlined Data Retrieval: Part Explorer Dashboard enabled operators to swiftly search and filter part data, reducing manual efforts and saving time.
  • Enhanced Decision-Making: Part Explorer Dashboard offered comprehensive insights into part structures and metrics, facilitating data-driven decision-making during product development.
  • Accurate Price Estimation: Price Estimation Dashboard improved accuracy and allowed users to access estimated prices for specific parts, fostering better planning and cost control.
  • Efficient Data Visualization: Data Upload Dashboard visualized uploaded part details, enabling users to make informed decisions and gain actionable insights.

Conclusion

The successful integration of Thingworx and Windchill showcased the potential of combining Industrial IoT and PLM platforms to revolutionize the manufacturing and automotive industries. The four interactive dashboards significantly improved data accessibility, decision-making capabilities, and cost estimation accuracy. By implementing this comprehensive solution, the company enhanced its operational efficiency, accelerated product development cycles, and gained a competitive edge in the market.
